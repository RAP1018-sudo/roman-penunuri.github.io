Fall 2025 · ECE 3317 · Solo

MATLAB transmission line calculator accepting load impedance, Γ, or VSWR+phase as input. Computes reflection coefficient, VSWR, percent power, Vmax/Vmin positions, input impedance at any distance, and plots the Smith chart with VSWR circle and standing wave pattern.

I grew up along the Gulf Coast, where hurricanes and extended power outages were part of life. Watching the grid fail — and watching it come back — gave me something more than curiosity: it gave me a reason to care about energy infrastructure that most people never think about until it’s gone.

That’s what pulled me into Electrical Engineering. I’m concentrated in Power Systems at UH’s Cullen College, and I’ve built out skills in embedded systems and software because real infrastructure problems don’t stay in one lane.

At Air Liquide I got to see what that looks like at scale — protective relay configuration, switchgear, industrial gas production running 24/7. On my own time I build PANTRi, a full-stack mobile app, solo, from scratch.

Looking for co-op and internship opportunities in power systems, defense, aerospace, MEP, circuit design, and R&D.
